Ahmed Faraz:

~The revolutionary poet, who refused to bow infront of dictators~

Ahmed Faraz (born Syed Ahmad Shah on January 12, 1931, in Kohat, died August 25, 2008) was one of the greatest modern Urdu poets of the 20th century. Renowned for his poignant romantic and revolutionary verses, he spent years in self-imposed exile during General Zia-ul-Haq's regime due to his vocal opposition to military dictatorships

One of his most famous couplet is :

Tum Takhalus Ko bhi akhlas smjhte ho faraz
Dost hota nhi har hath milane wala
